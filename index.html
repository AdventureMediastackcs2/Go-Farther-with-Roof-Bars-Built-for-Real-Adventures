Frequently Asked Questions

1. What’s the difference between roof bars and a roof rack?
 Roof bars are the base load-carrying cross members that attach directly to the vehicle or side rails. Roof racks are full platforms built atop those bars for additional cargo.

2. How much weight can these roof bars handle?
 The Baseline Overland Cross Bar supports substantial static and dynamic loads, typically accommodating rooftop tents and gear safely. Always check your vehicle’s roof weight limits.

3. Are the roof bars compatible with all vehicles?
 They’re designed for Baseline Overland rack systems but fit many vehicles with compatible mounting tracks. Fitment guidance is included.

4. Can the roof bars stay on year-round?
 Yes. Their corrosion-resistant coating allows for permanent installation in any season.

5. Do they require drilling?
 No drilling is needed for most setups. Mounting hardware fits existing rack or track slots.

6. How do I maintain them?
 Wipe with mild soap and water periodically; avoid abrasive cleaners to keep the finish pristine.
